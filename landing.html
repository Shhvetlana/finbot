The problem

Multi-country life is messy.
Existing tools weren't built for it.

Two bank accounts in Israel, one in France, a card in EUR, savings in USD. Statements in three languages, three formats, three portals. Every budgeting app assumes you live in one country with one currency.

🗂️

Spreadsheets break at scale

Manual entry works for one bank. It falls apart across countries, currencies, and partners sharing finances.

🌍

Apps don't cross borders

Most budgeting tools connect to banks in one country. They don't handle multi-currency life or mixed-language statements.

🤖

AI gives confident guesses

Generic AI assistants interpret raw bank data and produce plausible-sounding numbers. Real finance needs deterministic logic, not inference.

How Raisin works

From bank statement
to clear decision.

01

Upload your statements

Drop in bank statements from any of your accounts — PDF, CSV, XLSX. Raisin parses them regardless of bank format, language, or currency. Israeli banks, French banks, neobanks — all supported.

Multi-bank · Multi-language · Multi-currency
02

All logic runs in code, not in AI

Every calculation — spend totals, runway, category breakdowns, month-on-month trends — is computed deterministically in Python. No model interprets your numbers. Numbers are numbers.

Python computes · AI narrates
03

Once all data is pre-computed, the AI receives facts — not raw transactions — and writes a plain-language narrative: what changed, what's worth watching, what's fine.

No hallucinations · Only verified figures

Zero activation cost · Already in your pocket
Design principles

Built like a tool,
not like a product demo.

Friction is the enemy of adoption

If managing your finances requires logging into a new app, you won't. Raisin lives in Telegram because that's where you already are. The best financial tool is the one you actually use.

AI without logic is noise

AI is excellent at writing. It's unreliable at arithmetic. Raisin keeps these roles separate: Python handles every calculation, AI handles every sentence. Results are reproducible and auditable.

Calibration over alerts

An alert that fires every week trains you to ignore it. Raisin uses your historical baseline — not static rules — to surface only meaningful changes. Signal, not noise.

Your data stays yours

Statements are processed and discarded. No card numbers, no account IDs stored beyond what's needed. Built as a personal tool first — privacy was the default, not a feature added later.
